Our departments
Car body & Aero
The aero department handles the following areas:
  • Desingning and manufacturing body of the car;
  • Passenger ergonomics design;
  • Aerodynamics, strength of materials and weight optimization calculations;
  • Designing and manufacturing of composites;
  • Rapid prototyping.
The team's abilities
  • CAD designing, technical drawing (CATIA V6, SW);
  • CFD and FEA calculations (Altair Hyperworks, Ansys);
  • Lamination mould desinging and manufacturing;
  • Selection of materials, glues, fibers and resins;
  • CNC machining, lamination, general workshop skills.
